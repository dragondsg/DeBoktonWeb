The Nameless

An Intense Dytopia Men and Teenage Boys Love

Frazier sounds like the perfect society. The king is unknown, a regular citizen who has to live by the laws he makes, and the Nameless are the perfect peacekeepers. What could go wrong? Sound like famous last words? When Hanna's brother goes missing, Neil must uncover the corruption in the society to help her find them, but it may cost him his life. Men love this book. We have yet to find one who hasn't. Of course, many ladies love it too.

The Master Conspirators Series

Intense Intrigues to get the Throne!

The king has been murdered, and intrigues abound for those who want the throne. Friend has to fight against friend, or those they are fighting for will kill people they love.

The Last Dark Elf Series

If the Last Dark Elf is killed, Demons will be released, so he must live.

The Last Dark Elf's magic is keeping the world from being enslaved by the Slaugh, but an evil sorcerer wants to kill him and free them. This series was designed by a man, parts written by him, and definitely something men will love. Women will too.

Faeborn

Civilized War is Not Civil

Trained to fight in an arena to replace bloody battles, war mages have great abilities that they get by going on a soul quest through the Fae forest. Niam is suddenly thrown into leading a team where he is sworn to protect the kingdom and princess. To do so, he must make some unusual allies, including the daughter of the enemy.

Paladin

Super Powered Teenagers are Never Boring

Five super heroes, including the three most powerful, all go to the same school, but so does a notorious super villain. The heroes have to learn to work together to stop him. Michael always writes books guys will love.

Adventurers

Some Prices are Too High to Pay.

Becoming an adventurer is not easy. It requires discipline, training, and heart. Some want the power without the price and take short cuts that affect their souls. David, a true adventurer must find a way to stop the false adventurers before it's too late. This story is intense and creepy at times, but a very good read. Yes, it's written by Renee, but Michael came up with the story.
