Power Force Prep

A School for Powered People Like You've Never Seen Before

Power Force Prep is a school for powered people, one they are forced to go to. When Jay arrives, he finds his year students aren't getting the normal experience. With an increase in spore monsters, some bad enemies, and even rumors of aliens, the students are up against forces they never expected and danger they never imagined.

Fantasion

A Huge Twist on Isekai

Here, the hero can go between worlds, and must fix both worlds to save his fellow traveler from sure death. With in depth world buidling, creative races, fascinating plots, and great characters, this would make an amazing anime.

Faeborn

Civilized War is Not Civil

Trained to fight in an arena to replace bloody battles, war mages have great abilities that they get going on sould quest through the Fae forest. Niam is suddenly thrown into leading a team where he is sworn to protect the kingdom and princess. To do so, he must make some unusual allies, including the daughter of the enemy. This would be an amazing anime with all the different characters and war mage teams, the cool magic, and anime style fighting.

The CAMO Series

Dystopia meets Superhero with Epic Battles!

Only a few people in major cities survived the war due to chemical attacks, and those people would never be the same. Some admired their powers, but most feared them. Yet, without them, people would be murdered by those supposedly protecting them and beast would run rampant through the city. You'll love everything from Eccentrics, who plays epic battle music when they fight, to creative characters, to the epic fights.

Read the book. Don't wait for the movie, as much as we wish there was one in the works, there isn't.
