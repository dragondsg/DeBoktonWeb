Woodcreek Academy

You'll love reading this to your children!

Woodcreek Academy is a story of a school for non-humans, full of fun. Michael's sense of humor really comes through with zany characters and situations, but an interesting plot. The second grade class he wrote this for loved it, and older children love it too. Dads and moms enjoy reading this to their children. Make bedtime stories something everyone looks forward to with Woodcreek.

Counting Dragons

Don't Count Sheep. Count Dragons!

Count dragons as they go to sleep, and your children do too, with this adorable rhyme and beautiful artwork.

Sir Helps-a-lot and the Sad Princess

Sir Helps-a-Lot helps his sister, the Sad Princess, get to Sleep.

This is an adorable story written by a mother of four boys and a girl where a boy does everything he can to help his sad sister. Finally, she goes to sleep and is happy.

Sir Helps-a-Lot and the Messy Monster

Brothers can be Very Messy

Sir Helps-a-Lot helps his mom by making a game of cleaning up after his messy brother, the messy monster.

I Wonder

Were Mommy and Daddy Ever Children?

Children wonder what mommy and daddy were like before the children were born. Were mommy and daddy rock stars or butterflies? No, maybe they were little children too.

The teeny tiny Amargasaurus

The Teeny Tiny Amargasaurus Looks for a 5-Toed Dinosaur

This is imaginative and exciting, but repetitive enough, counting toes, to get to sleep. Spoiler, he doesn't find a 5-toed dinosaur. He finds something better.

Shingebiss

Cuddle up and Read this Wintery Tale

Shingebiss is an old Native American tale about a duck who, no matter how hard the North Wind tries to stop him does not give up.

The Creative Collection

Imaginative Rhymes that teach colors, opposites, and that being different isn't bad.

Colorful Dragons, Animal Opposites, and One Little Alien are cute rhymes that read quickly to eager children.

The Clown Collection

Cute Rhymes about Clowns.

Three rhymes that make quick stories when you are needing to do bedtime stories fast: Clowning Around, I'm Such a Clown, and Clowns with Shapes.

The Imagination Collection

Imaginative Rhymes to Encourage Creativity

No Need to be Bored encourages imaginative play on a rainy day. Magic Carpet is a fun trip on a blanket. In My Imagination children can see that imagination adds fun to life.
