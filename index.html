From Our Family to Yours!

Everyone is different, but there’s a story for everyone. Whether you want something dark and gritty, a classic fantasy adventure, a bedtime story, or anything in between, we have fiction for everyone; all with the values we want to share with our own family. Let our family’s wild imaginations bring joy to you and yours.

Go On an Adventure with Us!

Stop doomscrolling yourself into malaise and come join one of our protagonists on an exciting adventure! Instead of lamenting how your life compares to the photoshopped lives online, be grateful you don’t have to be a Nameless. Instead of wishing you were more exciting, discover the exciting life of a War Mage-and the problems involved. Find your hero to give you the motivation to fight through your life, all in the pages of a book.

Listen Up!

Not everyone has time to relax with a good book: that’s why we have audiobooks for those who don’t want to run into things while reading and walking. (We’ve had that problem before) We have books and audiobooks available in every genre we can think of and a few we made up ourselves, just so we can reach an audience as varied as our ideas.

For Your Entertainment.

For our family, writing is more than just a hobby. It’s the way an individual expresses themselves. It’s the way two totally different ideas from totally different minds merge together to form something new. And it’s a collaboration between family members near and distant to bring us closer together. Every story we write is for us; but it’s also for you! We want you to share in the banter between brother and sister, to experience the joy of having your mother finally understand your explanation, and to see the worlds that we’ve so meticulously crafted for you. To become part of our family’s ongoing legacy, just pick up a book!

Find Your Hero!

Life can stink. You may not be attacked by a dragon or called on to overthrow the tyrant, but you do face challenges that can sometimes feel overwhelming. Escape into a story and find a hero that resonates with you; then come back with the courage, the motivation, the idea, or the love that you need to overcome your own challenges. Here at DeBokton, we believe that everyone has a hero inside them, and we want you to find yours! Join us on our journey to find your hero today!
