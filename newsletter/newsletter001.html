Welcome to the DeBokton Family

Your adventure with us starts here. You won't be asked to slay dragons, open a bunch of spam, or even cook dinner for the family -- Definitely vacuuming is out. You can sit back and enjoy our newsletter. If you like it, invite your friends to join. If you don't, invite your enemies.

What is DeBokton Book?

We have younger reader series and children's picture books. But if you want a place to get started, you can get book 1 of this popular series for just 99 cents!

Writing Tip:

Interested in writing? Our newsletter will give good tips.

Today's tip: If you don't have an idea of what to write, start asking what-if questions. What if you suddenly found out your entire life is a computer simulation? What if thoughts became real? What if you woke up with super powers? What-if question can lead to a start of a book idea. What if squirrels armed with sporks suddenly started rebelling and tried to take over the world? -- Ok, some are better than others.

If you need an idea or just want to enjoy listening to us come up with them, check out Story Ideas on the Fly, where we pull a random prompt from a hat and develop it. You're welcome to use any of those ideas. Ideas are free.
